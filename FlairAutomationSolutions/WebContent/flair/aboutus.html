Mission and Vision

Inception

Flair Automation Solutions is a Pune based organization started in May 2014. We provide solutions for automotive body welding lines with expertise in Process, Mechanical & Controls Design, Assembly, Integration, Installation and Project Management.

We, at flair, intend to grow as a integrator for automotive body shops by providing world class solutions.
